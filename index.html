Program tracks

Three tracks that build from fundamentals to production readiness.

Foundations

Approx. 4 weeks

  • Python for data: Pandas, NumPy, data cleaning
  • Exploratory analysis & visualization
  • Statistics & experimentation basics

Modeling

Approx. 6 weeks

  • Classical ML: regression, trees/ensembles, SVM
  • Deep learning: CNNs, RNNs/LSTMs, transformers
  • NLP/GenAI, evaluation, and model diagnostics

ML Engineering

Approx. 4 weeks

  • Pipelines, feature stores, and experiment tracking
  • CI/CD for models, containerization, APIs
  • Monitoring, drift detection, cost & performance tuning
